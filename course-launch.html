What 60 Hours Actually Looks Like

You'll complete 60 hours at your placement over the semester — most students land around 4-6 hours a week. Every week, you'll submit a projected schedule (Sunday before) and your actual hours (Sunday after) through the weekly log, plus a short reflection. Your dashboard tracks your running total against the 60-hour goal automatically.
